Core Programs

Essential water safety skills designed to build confidence and stay with your child for a lifetime.

ISR lessons are one-on-one and tailored to your child’s skill and comfort level. 6–7 week session, 4 days per week (Monday–Thursday), 10 minutes per day.

Self-Rescue® Infant

CHILDREN 6 MONTHS TO 18 MONTHS OLD

Duration: 6 – 7 weeks

Infants learn the essential self-rescue skill of rolling onto their back to float, rest, and breathe. They are taught to stay calm and maintain this position independently until help arrives—giving them a critical layer of safety in the water.

View pricing View schedule

Self-Rescue® Toddler

CHILDREN 18 MONTHS TO 8 YEARS OLD

Duration: 6 – 7 weeks

Children learn the swim-float-swim sequence, for reaching safety. They swim with their face in the water, roll onto their back to rest and breathe. They continue swimming until they reach the wall, steps, or edge.
