The Mission
(Why it Matters)

Drowning is silent. It happens faster than you think. Most accidents don't look like what you imagine. No splashing. No screaming. Just seconds. And it doesn't only happen in deep water or crowded pools. It happens where kids feel safest — at home, in everyday moments, when barriers fail. That's why supervision alone isn't enough. That's why fences aren't enough.

Survival skills aren't optional — they're essential.
